About Us

Home to the world famous Triple Coconut Cream Pie, Hansom Rob’ Bakery offers artisan breads, handmade pastries, and cakes which are baked daily in small batches. Breakfast and lunch options created by the chef team, start at breakfast with bacon and organic eggs and finish the day with a quart of tomato soup and a loaf of fresh bread.
